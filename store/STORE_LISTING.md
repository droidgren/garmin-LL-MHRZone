## App Name

Last Lap Max HR

## Short Description

Shows the maximum heart rate zone from your last completed lap.

## Full Description

Last Lap Max HR is a Connect IQ data field for interval-focused training.

It tracks the peak heart rate of your most recently completed lap and shows that result as a decimal zone value (for example, `4.7` = 70% through zone 4).

### Highlights

- Uses your heart rate zone configuration from your Garmin profile
- Updates immediately after each lap event (manual or auto-lap)
- Shows `--` until your first lap is completed
- Optional Garmin-style HR zone digit colors with an on-device toggle (default ON)
- Resets cleanly when the activity timer is reset

### Typical Use Cases

- Verify peak effort reached target zone during work intervals
- Compare maximum intensity across repeated intervals
- Monitor recovery laps to confirm HR stayed below a threshold zone
- Spot cardiac drift by watching peak HR zone rise across later laps
